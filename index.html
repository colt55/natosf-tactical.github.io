Announcement

HEADS UP, OPERATORS:
NATO SF Tactical is brand new—and yeah, the server’s emptier than your last bottle of Jack, but that’s how we like it.

Here’s the standard: You’re not just some trigger-happy conscript. You’re a walking toolbox, and you better know how to use every wrench.

Every player is expected to be:

  • Combat Medic: Advanced Combat Medicine (ACM) medical system is mandatory. If you can’t handle full ACE/ACM medical, triage, airway management, or field surgery, you’ll die more than you respawn.
  • Combat Engineer & EOD: Fix, build, blow, or disable. IEDs are your bitch, not your excuse.
  • Interpreter: If you can’t eavesdrop and interpret Russian or Taliban chatter, Google Translate is free—use it.
  • Pilot: If you can’t fly a helicopter nap-of-the-earth under fire, uninstall now.
  • Sniper/Recon: Master the G29 .338, know your range tables, and do real recon—spot, mark, and coordinate.
  • Recon Specialist: Get eyes on, send coords, and make it count. Nobody is holding your hand here.
  • Radio Operator: ACRE2 short/long range radios, building antennas, keeping comms up, and using the support system (Simplex Support Services)—no room for “uh, how do I use this?”

Can’t keep up? You want extra training?
Contact Colt on Steam for a lesson—don’t bug the rest of us in the middle of a firefight.

No Discord, no drama. TeamSpeak only. English only. Germany-based server.
